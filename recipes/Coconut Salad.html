Coconut and Cucumber Salad

A refreshing clash of flavors

The coconut cucumber salad is packed with the perfect amount of zest and flavor, with a unique texture that goes perfectly with smoked fish. This healthy meal might seem a bit daunting to put together, but the end result is sooo worth it!

Ingredients

  • 1 large coconut
  • 1.5 large cucumbers
  • 1 red chilli, deseeded
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 30g fresh coriander/cilantro
  • 50g radishes
  • 300ml tinned coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon caster sugar
  • 10g salt
  • 10g cracked black pepper

Steps

  1. Crack open a coconut and scrap out the flesh with knife. Cracking open the coconut in a sandwich bag by cracking it with a knife makes things much less messy.
  2. Grate the flesh with a grater, and toss it all in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Skin the cucumbers, slice them in half longways, scoop out the fleshy middle with a spoon (discard it), then slice it into 2 inch cubes. This too goes into the mixing bowl.
  4. Scrape the seeds out from the red chili and discard, then cut it finely, and toss in the bowl.
  5. Slice half the radishes thinly, and grate the other half, putting both into the mixing bowl.
  6. Roughly cut the coriander/cilantro and mix into the bowl along with salt and pepper. Mix the salad with your hands thoroughly.
  7. Pour coconut milk from the can along with juice from 1/2 a lemon, a whole lime's worth of juice, and a tbsp of sugar. Stir this concoction up with a spoon.
  8. Pour your newly mixed salad dressing over your salad as much as you'd like, then plate it up and serve. Enjoy!
